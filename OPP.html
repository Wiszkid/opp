About Us

Welcome to Other Peoples Pets, the premier pet care service in Brooklyn. Our dedicated team of professional dog walkers and caregivers is committed to providing top-quality, loving care for your furry family members while you're away. Based in Red Hook, Brooklyn, we proudly serve the surrounding neighborhoods and have been in business since 2002, earning a reputation as the trusted neighborhood pet care service.

At Other People's Pets, we understand that your pets are part of your family and deserve the very best care. Our staff is passionate about animals and will treat your pets with the love and attention they deserve. Whether you need daily dog walks, pet sitting, or overnight care, we're here to provide reliable and personalized service that meets your specific needs.

We take pride in offering a safe and comfortable environment for your pets, and we're committed to building long-lasting relationships with both pets and their owners. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you care for your furry family members.
